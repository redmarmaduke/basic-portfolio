About Me

I was born in Napa, California and raised in the Central Valley as a first generation Portuguese-American.


My interests include symphonic and marching music, computer graphics, all spicy foods of the Indian, Ethiopian, and Central American varieties; and certain types of computer games. I cook some Indian dishes at home and unfortunately have to eat out for the other two cuisines. I used to play semi-professionally in symphonic and marching bands. I enjoy computer graphics theory and have a toy ray-tracer written in C++ and Java. The C++ variant can be compiled to run on CUDA devices. I am a fan of certain types of computer games including first person looters and massively multi-player online role playing games (MMORPGs). Additionally, I was a casual practitioner of Praying Mantis Kung Fu for approximately a decade under my late master Paul Eng.
